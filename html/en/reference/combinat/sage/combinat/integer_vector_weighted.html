sage.combinat.integer_vector_weighted.WeightedIntegerVectors(n=None, weight=None)¶

Returns the combinatorial class of integer vectors of n weighted by weight, that is, the nonnegative integer vectors \((v_1,\dots,v_{length(weight)})\) satisfying \(\sum_i v_i weight[i]==n\).

INPUT:

  • n – a non negative integer (optional)
  • weight – a tuple (or list or iterable) of positive integers
